Etrian Odyssey V: Beyond the Myth[a] is a dungeon crawler role-playing video game developed and published by Atlus for the Nintendo 3DS. The game was released in Japan in August 2016, in North America in October 2017,[1] and in the PAL region the following month.

Shigeo Komori returned as director of the game, after Daisuke Kaneda directed Etrian Odyssey IV.[2] Komori had previously directed both Etrian Odyssey II and Etrian Odyssey III.

Etrian Odyssey V was well received by critics,[3] and was the eighth highest rated Nintendo 3DS game of 2017 on the review aggregator Metacritic.[11]

The game was nominated for "Handheld Game of the Year" at the 21st Annual D.I.C.E. Awards.[12]
